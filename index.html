Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a Digital Pass? +
Navigate to the 'Tickets' tab in the main sidebar, select your preferred route and stops, click 'Book Digital Pass', select a mock UPI app, and authorize the simulated payment to generate your pass.
What is Low-Bandwidth Mode? +
Low-Bandwidth mode disabled heavy map loading and graphic tiles. It switches to a highly optimized, lightweight text-based arrivals board, ensuring accessibility even under poor 2G/3G network conditions.
How accurate are the ETA countdowns? +
The ETAs are calculated dynamically in real time. We simulate current bus speeds, stop dwell times, and routes congestion values to ensure high precision countdown timers.
